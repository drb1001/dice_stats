Dice Stats

Add some dice rolls to compare stats

Type your dice rolls as you would in Roll20. Most of the same syntax is supported (more info)

  • Standard dice rolls (d2, d3, d4, d6, d8, d10, d12,d 20) upto 10 at a time, eg: 10d6
  • Advantage and disadvantage rolls (rolling 2 dice) - use kh/dh/kl/dl for keep/drop highest/lowest, eg: 2d20kh
  • A special roll used in 5e starting stats rolls - roll 4 d6 and remove the lowest, ie: 4d6dl
  • Modifiers on all of the above between -10 and +10 (written at the end), eg: 2d20kh+4
  • In Progress.. Rerolling specfic numbers (like brutal weapons in D&D 4e), eg: 8d6r<1
  • In Progress.. Rerolling once for specfic numbers (like great weapon fighting in D&D 5e), eg: 3d10ro<2
